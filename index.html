Marching Spotlight!

One way to get better at Marching Band is by watching other Marching Bands that possess the "next-level" skills that we are trying to obtain! Marching Spotlight features various spectacular High School Marching Bands, College Bands, DCI groups, and possibly even WGI performances!

Year: 2024

Show Name: Mondriesque

Group: Avon HS

Location: ISSMA Championships

Experience Level: High School

Context: Mondriesque was a bold fusion of musical performance and visual art, drawn from the geometric abstraction of Dutch modernist Piet Mondrian. The show featured the performers dressed like Mondrian paintings and several cubes painted with primary colors scattered across the field. Mondriesque's intricacy created an exhilarating narrative that placed them 1st at ISSMA Championships and at BOA Grand National Finals with a score of 97.300.
